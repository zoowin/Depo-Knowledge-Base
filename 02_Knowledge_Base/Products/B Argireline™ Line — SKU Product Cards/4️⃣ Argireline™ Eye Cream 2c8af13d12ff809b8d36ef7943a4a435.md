# 4️⃣ Argireline™ Eye Cream

### **1. One-liner**

An eye cream formulated with Argireline™ to support smoother-looking, well-hydrated under-eye skin.

---

### **2. Who It’s For**

- 眼周干纹、细纹
- 日常眼周护理需求
- 需要温和、长期使用方案的人群

---

### **3. Core Benefits**

- Helps reduce the appearance of fine lines
- Improves hydration and comfort
- Supports a smoother under-eye appearance

---

### **4. Hero Ingredients & Why**

- **Argireline™**：针对表情纹外观
- **Moisturizing agents**：提升眼周含水量，缓解干纹

---

### **5. How to Use**

- AM & PM
- 于眼部精华后使用
- 可搭配 Eye Stick 或 Micro-dart Patch

---

### **6. Pairing & Conflicts**

- ✅ 推荐搭配：Eye Stick、MPS Serum
- ❌ 避免叠加刺激性产品

---

### **7. Messaging Angles**

- “眼周护理需要持续，而不是一次性”
- “温和配方，适合每天使用”

---

### **8. Cross-sell Logic**

- Eye Cream + Eye Stick（日常 + 即时）
- Eye Cream + Micro-dart Patch（维稳 + 加强）
