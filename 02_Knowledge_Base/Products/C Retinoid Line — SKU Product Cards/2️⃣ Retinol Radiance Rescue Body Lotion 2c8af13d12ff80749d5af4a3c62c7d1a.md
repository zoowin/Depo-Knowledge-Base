# 2️⃣ Retinol Radiance Rescue Body Lotion

### **One-liner**

A body lotion combining **retinol and AHA** to gently exfoliate, smooth skin texture, boost radiance, and deeply hydrate body skin.

---

### **Who It’s For**

- Body 肤质粗糙、暗沉或细纹显现的人群（如腿部、手臂）
- 需要改善身体肌肤质感与光泽的人
- 想用夜间乳液级护理提升体感肌肤状态的人

---

### **Core Benefits**

- Gently smooths and renews skin surface
- Enhances radiance and texture
- Deeply hydrates and supports long-lasting moisture

---

### **Hero Ingredients & Why**

- **Retinol**：支持改善肌肤表面纹理
- **AHA (Alpha Hydroxy Acid)**：温和去除老废角质，提升光泽
- **Collagen & Cholesterol**：帮助滋养与强化皮肤屏障

---

### **How to Use**

- 全身清洁干燥皮肤上涂抹
- 轻柔按摩直至吸收
- 建议夜间使用较好；白天一定要用防晒

---

### **Pairing & Conflicts**

- ✅ 推荐搭配：Retinol Night Cream（面部韵律统一），Cica Body 系列
- ❌ 避免与过度去角质类身体产品叠加使用
