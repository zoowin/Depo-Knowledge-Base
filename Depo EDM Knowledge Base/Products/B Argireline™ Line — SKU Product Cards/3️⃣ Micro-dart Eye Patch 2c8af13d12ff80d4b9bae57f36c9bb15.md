# 3️⃣ Micro-dart Eye Patch

### **1. One-liner**

A non-invasive micro-dart eye patch designed to deliver active ingredients where the skin needs them most.

---

### **2. Who It’s For**

- 眼周细纹明显人群
- 想要**短周期集中护理**的用户
- 对注射类医美犹豫或排斥的人群

---

### **3. Core Benefits**

- Targets under-eye fine lines
- Supports visible smoothing of the under-eye area
- Non-invasive, at-home treatment option

---

### **4. Hero Technology & Why**

- **Micro-dart technology**：帮助有效成分精准递送
- **Peptide-based actives**：支持眼周细纹外观改善

---

### **5. How to Use**

- PM 使用
- 清洁后贴敷于眼下
- 建议周期性使用（非每日）

---

### **6. Pairing & Conflicts**

- ✅ 推荐搭配：Eye Cream、Cica Line
- ❌ 使用当晚避免叠加强刺激成分
