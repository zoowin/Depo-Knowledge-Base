# 1️⃣ Argireline™ MPS Serum

### **1. One-liner**

A targeted peptide serum formulated with Argireline™ to help reduce the appearance of expression lines and fine wrinkles.

---

### **2. Who It’s For**

- 关注表情纹 / 动态纹的人群
- 眼周、额头、法令纹等**局部护理**需求
- 不考虑或不适合医美注射的用户

---

### **3. Core Benefits**

- Helps reduce the appearance of expression lines
- Supports smoother-looking skin in high-movement areas
- Lightweight texture suitable for daily use

---

### **4. Hero Ingredients & Why**

- **Argireline™**：被广泛研究的六胜肽，支持表情纹外观改善
- **Peptide complex**：协同支持肌肤平滑度与细腻度

### **6. How to Use**

- AM & PM
- 使用顺序：Cleanse → **MPS Serum** → 后续精华 / 面霜
- 重点涂抹于表情纹集中区域（眼周、额头、法令纹）

### **7. Pairing & Conflicts**

- ✅ 推荐搭配：Matrixyl® Line、Cica Line
- ❌ 避免夸大与医美效果对比
