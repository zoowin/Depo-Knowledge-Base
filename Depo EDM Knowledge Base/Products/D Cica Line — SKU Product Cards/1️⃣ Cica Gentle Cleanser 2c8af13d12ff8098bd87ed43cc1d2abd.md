# 1️⃣ Cica Gentle Cleanser

### **1. One-liner**

A gentle, barrier-friendly cleanser formulated with Cica to cleanse without stripping the skin.

---

### **2. Who It’s For**

- 敏感肌 / 易泛红肌肤
- 抗老护理期间需要温和清洁的人群
- 不耐受强清洁或起泡型洁面的用户

---

### **3. Core Benefits**

- Gently cleanses without disrupting the skin barrier
- Helps calm and comfort sensitive skin
- Leaves skin feeling clean yet balanced

---

### **4. Hero Ingredients & Why**

- **Centella Asiatica (Cica)**：帮助舒缓、支持屏障
- **Mild surfactants**：降低清洁刺激风险

---

### **5. How to Use**

- AM & PM
- 日常洁面第一步
- 特别适合搭配 Retinoid / Micro-dart 护理周期

---

### **6. Pairing & Conflicts**

- ✅ 推荐搭配：Retinoid Line、Argireline™ Line、Matrixyl Line
- ❌ 不作为深度清洁或去角质产品定位

---

### **7. Messaging Angles**

- “清洁不该是刺激的开始”
- “温和清洁，是抗老的第一道防线”
- “给敏感肌一个更友好的起点”

---

### **8. Cross-sell Logic**

- Cleanser + Cica Serum（基础修护）
- Cleanser + Retinoid Routine（温和起步）
