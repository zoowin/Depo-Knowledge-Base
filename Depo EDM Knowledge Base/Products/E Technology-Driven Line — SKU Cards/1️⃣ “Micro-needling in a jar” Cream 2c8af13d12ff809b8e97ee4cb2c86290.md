# 1️⃣ “Micro-needling in a jar” Cream

## **1️⃣ One-liner（一句话定位）**

A technology-driven cream designed to mimic the benefits of micro-needling through advanced, non-invasive delivery — all in a jar.

---

## **2️⃣ Who It’s For（适合谁）**

- 对 **micro-needling / skin needling** 感兴趣，但不想做医美的人
- 想要更明显效果，但对注射、仪器或恢复期有顾虑的用户
- 对“技术型护肤”有兴趣、愿意了解原理的客户
- 已有基础护肤经验，希望 **升级护肤方式** 的人群

---

## **3️⃣ Core Benefits（核心价值，而不是功效承诺）**

- Enhances the delivery of active ingredients through advanced formulation technology
- Supports smoother-looking skin and improved overall skin appearance
- Offers a **non-invasive alternative** to device-based treatments
- No needles, no downtime, suitable for at-home use

> ⚠️ 重点：
> 
> 
> **外观改善 + 递送效率**
> 

---

## **4️⃣ Hero Technology & Why（核心技术逻辑）**

- **Advanced delivery technology**
    
    Designed to help active ingredients reach where the skin can best utilize them
    
- Inspired by micro-needling concepts, **without puncturing the skin**
- Allows users to experience a “tech-forward” skincare approach at home

> 核心卖点不是“成分多强”，而是：
> 

> how it works, not just what’s inside
> 

---

## **5️⃣ Proof / Claims Boundary（合规边界）**

- Non-invasive, topical skincare product
- No medical devices, no needles
- Claims focus on **appearance-level improvement** and **delivery support**
- Avoid comparisons implying equivalence to in-office micro-needling

---

## **6️⃣ How to Use（使用方式）**

- AM or PM
- Apply to clean skin as a treatment step
- Can be followed by moisturizer if needed
- Suitable for routine use without recovery time

---

## **7️⃣ Pairing & Conflicts（搭配逻辑）**

- ✅ 推荐搭配：
    - Matrixyl® Line（基础抗老）
    - Cica Line（稳定与修护）

- ❌ 不需要搭配任何仪器或滚针工具
