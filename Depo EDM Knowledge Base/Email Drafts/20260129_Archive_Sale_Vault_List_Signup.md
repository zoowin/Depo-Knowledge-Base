# Email Campaign: Archive Sale Vault List Signup 2026

> Logic Breakdown
>
> 1. Title
>    * Concept: Vault list / 名单先行。
>    * Hook: 先加入名单，再收到专属 Archive Sale 页面链接。
> 2. Body
>    * Focus: 不直接讲折扣或单品，只讲「进入页面」需要先上名单。
>    * Role of this email: 唯一目的就是把读者放进 Klaviyo 里的 Archive / Vault segment。
> 3. Offer Positioning
>    * 以静默、克制的方式提出「提前进入」的机会。
>    * 把名单说成一种安静、偏内圈的选择，而不是强迫性促销。

Campaign Info
* Name: Archive Sale Vault List Signup 2026 (Internal)
* Type: Promotional / List-Building Teaser
* Theme: The Vault List – 名单先于页面开启
* Funnel: Warm / VIP-leaning segments
* Goal: 收集 Archive Sale 专属名单到 Klaviyo，用于后续发送专属 Archive 页面链接

---

Subject Line Options
1. The Depology Archive opens soon. Join the Vault list first.
2. Your key to our Archive Sale starts with this list.
3. Be on the list when the Depology Vault opens.

Preview Text
Join the early-access list now. We’ll send your private link when the Archive Sale page opens.

---

Email Content

Hero Image
Visual: 延续 Archive / Vault 的情绪：静物、柔和光线、少量产品或细节出现在画面中，好像仓内的一部分被轻轻照亮。重点是“入口感”，而不是折扣或具体 SKU。

Headline
The Archive is opening.
Unlock it with the Vault List.

Subheadline
Join the list to secure your access key.

Body Copy (Simplified & Connected)
Every year, we open the Depology Archive.
This event is hosted inside "The Vault"—a private page hidden from the main site.

To get inside, you need to be on the Vault List.

Add your name now and we’ll:

– Send you the private link to enter the Vault as soon as it opens
– Give you early access to shop the Archive collection
– Secure your spot before the doors open to everyone else

This email is your invitation to the list.
The key to the Archive will follow later.

Call to Action
Button Text: JOIN THE VAULT LIST  
Link URL: [Insert Klaviyo signup / preference page URL]

Supporting Note (Optional, Near Footer)
Once the Archive page opens, it will only be available for a limited time.  
Join the list now so you don’t miss the link.

---

Visual Direction Notes for Design
* Overall Style: 延续“Vault Edition” 语气，安静、克制、略偏 editorial，而不是「促销横幅」。
* Color Palette: Warm grey、soft beige、低饱和金属色，强化档案室 / 金库氛围。
* Typography: 标题可用气质更明显的 serif 或轻量无衬线，正文保持清晰易读。
* Layout: 首屏包含 hero 图 + Headline + Subheadline + 主 CTA；正文中段可再次出现文本形式的 CTA。
* Archive Motif: 可保留细框、标签条、抽屉或档案卡等轻微暗示，继续讲 “Archive” 的故事但不过度装饰。
