# 2026 Archive Sale (The Vault) Campaign Strategy

## 1. Campaign Overview
*   **Theme:** "The Vault Opens" / Archive Sale
*   **Concept:** Once a year, we open our private archive. This is not a "clearance bin" sale; it is a rare opportunity to access discontinued favorites, final batches, and limited runs.
*   **Primary Goal:** Revenue (clearing older inventory) + List Building (Early Access signups).
*   **Tone:** Exclusive, editorial, urgent but sophisticated. "Museum Archive" aesthetic.

## 4. Offer & Product Strategy

### Discount Logic
Instead of a flat % off, use **Tiered "Archive" Pricing** to build baskets:
*   **"The $10 Vault"**: Entry-level impulse buys (Sheet masks, near-expiry serums).
*   **"The $25 Collection"**: Core serums with old packaging.
*   **"The 50% Off Sets"**: Bundles of discontinued lines.

### Key Products (Based on Inventory)
1.  **Retinal Serum ($10 Hero):** The main hook. High perceived value, deep discount.
2.  **Discontinued Sets:** "Last chance to own."
3.  **Imperfect Box Stock:** "Perfect product, imperfect box."

### Rules
*   **Final Sale:** No returns/exchanges (standard for Archive sales).
*   **Limits:** Max 3 per customer (creates scarcity).
