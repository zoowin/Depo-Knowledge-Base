2. All year promotion plan

### Q1 Promotions

### January

1. **The January Sale (December 26 – January 12/18) [Essential]**
    1. A major post-holiday clearance event to discount overstocked items.
    2. Effective for driving high-margin sales with repeat customers.
    3. End date can vary depending on inventory needs**.**

### February

1. **Valentines Day (February 14th) [Optional]**
    1. Recommended as a weekend sale with a flat discount of 15% or 20%.

### March

1. **Archive Sale(February or March) [Recommended]**
    1. Targeted **VIP-only sale** for **excess inventory or expiring stock**.
    2. Customers **must sign up in advance** to access the sale.
    3. **Outreach begins 1-2 weeks before launch** to drive sign-ups.
2. **St Patricks Day Day (March 17th) [Optional]**
    1. Recommended as a weekend sale with a flat discount of 15% or 20%.Q2 Promotions

### Q2 Promotions

### April

1. **Easter Weekend (April 20th) [Optional]**
    1. Recommended as a **weekend sale** with a **flat discount of 15% or 20%**.

### May

1. **Mothers Day (May 11th) [Optional]**
    1. Recommended as a **weekend sale** with a **flat discount of 15% or 20%**.
2. **Memorial Weekend (May 22-27th)[ Essential]**
    1. Runs **from the Thursday before Memorial Day until the Tuesday after**.
    2. Focuses on **clearing overstock** and driving **repeat customer sales**.

### June

1. **Fathers Day (June 15th) [Optional]**
2. Recommended as a **weekend sale** with a **flat discount of 15% or 20%**.

### Q3 Promotions

### July

1. **4th of July Sale (July 1–8) [Essential]**
    1. Major promotional event competing with **Amazon sales**.
    2. Focuses on **discounting overstock and high-margin repeat sales**.
    3. Recommended to **offer strong deals** to stay competitive.

### September

1. **Labor Day Sale (September 1) [Essential]**
    1. Significant discount event: **20% or 25% off**.
    2. A great opportunity to **clear inventory before Q4**.

### Q4 Promotions

### October

1. **Halloween Discount (October 31) [Essential]**
    1. Recommended as a **weekend sale** with a **flat discount of 15% or 20%**.

### November - Black Friday & Cyber Week – The Most Important Sale

1. **Black Friday Early Access (November 26–27)**
    1. **Soft promotions** can begin as early as **November 17**, but avoid discounting **best-sellers** early.
    2. Influencer partnerships can help **build anticipation**.
    3. **Exclusive Early Access Sale** for subscribers who sign up in advance.
        1. Drive urgency with **"limited quantities available"** or **one-time discounts**.
        2. Unique early access discounts (e.g., **80-90% off select items**, **new product at 50% off**) build hype.
            1. Start email outreach 2-3 weeks in advance
            2. Ask subscribers to sign up to a list
            3. If they subscribe they will receive Early Access
2. **Black Friday Sale (November 28+)**
    1. Uses the **same sale page as Early Access**.
    2. **Exclusive offers expire at midnight**, creating **urgency and FOMO**.
    3. Keeping some items **marked as "sold out" from Early Access** reinforces demand.
3. **Cyber Week (December 1–7)**
    1. Introduce **daily offers** to **combat sales fatigue**.
    2. Hold back select deals **from Black Friday** to create **fresh promotions**.
    3. Use **additional 15-20% off** to push remaining inventory at the end of the week.

### December

1. **Winter Clearance Sale (December 26 – January 12/18)**
    1. Similar to The January Sale, focused on overstock clearance.
    2. Effective in driving repeat customer purchases.
    3. End date can be adjusted based on sales performance.

Q&A 环节

1. Evergreen和Sales之间的占比有一些要求吗？
    1. Sales按照上面的all year promotion plan做就行
        1. Small sale
            1. Ex. Easter / valentine - 直接给一个discout code for 全站
            2. 占比少，通常就是周末两天两封邮件，剩下就是evergreen
        2. Middle sale
            1. Ex. Archive sale, memorial sale, july 4th sale
            2. 持续时间大约一周左右，5-8封邮件，剩下就是evergreen
        3. Big sale
            1. Ex. Black Friday, winter sale
            2. 占比高，在11月中下旬到1月中旬，比例会在60% - 80%
2. Evergreen中里面的Topical content和Product focused之间怎么分配，内容是自己想的对吧？有要求吗
    1. Topical 可以占到70%-80%
    2. Product foucsed 看情况，如果你有想要强调的单品或者内容，就可以写
    3. 内容是自己想的
3. 我们有没有数据上的统计关于最大收入类型的产品，8大高兴趣产品是否轮流来就行了。有时间上的要求嘛，比如季节，或者根据之前的销量情况预估。
    1. 最大收入类型这个没有统计，应该是两个精华液的3f2
    2. Sales 上面offer一般这样做
        1. Matrixyl 3000 serum & peptide complex serum 每年，每个大sale都做3f2
        2. Micro needle patch: up to 45% off (for 16 pairs)
        3. Matriplex cream & peptide eye cream: 30-40% off
        4. 上面是best sellers, 其他产品的offer可以自己定，best seller offer 每年没有太大的变化
