## 1. Hero Image (Concept)
**Context:** Visualizing the transition from heavy winter skincare to light spring routine.
**Subject:** A fresh, organized bathroom shelf or texture comparison.

```text
Professional commercial photography, high resolution, 4k. A bright, airy composition representing "Spring Cleaning for Skincare". 

Scene: A clean, minimalist white bathroom shelf or vanity surface. 
Key Elements: 
1. Fresh botanical elements (soft green leaves or spring pink flowers) in the background, out of focus.
2. A visual comparison of textures: A heavy, thick cream jar (perhaps closed or pushed to the back/side) vs. a lightweight, glowing, translucent serum (front and center, catching the light).
Lighting: Soft, natural morning light, creating a fresh and awakening atmosphere.
Color Palette: White, Soft Sage Green, Pale Pink.
Style: Clinical luxury, clean, modern, editorial skincare photography.
```

## 2. Product Trio (The Spring Swap)
**Context:** The 3 specific products recommended for the spring routine.
**Subject:** Opuntia Cleansing Balm, Argireline Serum, Cica Serum.

```text
Professional product photography, studio shot, 4k. A balanced trio composition of three skincare products on a clean, white or pale stone surface.

Product 1 (Left): A yellow/orange cleansing balm in a jar, lid off to show the rich, melting texture.
Product 2 (Center): A silver/metallic dropper bottle (serum), elegant and sleek.
Product 3 (Right): A green glass serum bottle, translucent and soothing.

Lighting: Soft, diffused studio lighting with subtle botanical shadows (leaf shadows) cast across the scene to suggest spring sunlight.
Style: Minimalist, high-end beauty brand aesthetic, sharp focus on product textures and packaging.
```
